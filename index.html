DURABILITY

Seed sowing robots are exposed to various environmental conditions, including rough terrains, moisture, dust, and temperature fluctuations. Therefore, high-quality materials that are durable and resistant to wear and tear are essential. This includes robust metal or composite materials for the frame, chassis, and other structural components that can withstand the rigors of farming operations.
